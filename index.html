About Me

My name is Maria, I'm new to coding. I am a student at Denver University Coding Bootcamp program and work hard to learn coding basics. My background is in linguistics but I've always been interested in computers. I tried coding a little bit but was convinced that it is for boys (gender stereotypes are so strong in Russia). Now I'm older and understand that it is not about gender at all. It is what you can do and how you apply your knowledge. Coding is exciting!


My husband, my daughter and I live in Colorado and we love it. The amount of snow and sun is perfect here. We do a lot of skiing, snowboarding and hiking. We used to live in Russia (originally), Boston and New York. After our daughter was born we decided to move where the sky is bigger and the mountains are closer so Denver became our home.
